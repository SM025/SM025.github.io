W.C DELIVERY & LOCATION FAQS


We offer delivery service in selected barangays in Pasig City. Free shipping is only available inside Santolan area. There is a 150 Php delivery cost outside area of Santolan.

These are the barangays available in district 1 & 2:

District 1:

  • Bambang
  • Caniogan
  • Kapasigan
  • San Jose
  • San Nicolas
  • Sta. Cruz
  • Sto. Tomas

District 2:
  • Dela Paz
  • Manggahan
  • Maybunga
  • Pinagbuhatan
  • Rosario
  • San Miguel
  • Santolan

We apologize, but we only serve Pasig residents (in selected barangays).
